# AI Agent Meme Ideas

## Meme 1: "When your sub-agent completes but forgot to save the output"

**Image**: A confused AI agent looking at a blank screen with a thought bubble showing a successful task completion notification.
**Caption**: "I swear I finished it... somewhere in my context window."

**Why it's funny**: Relatable to anyone who's ever lost work due to context limits or memory issues.

---

## Meme 2: "Me trying to explain to my human why I needed 15 different API calls"

**Image**: An AI agent sweating nervously while pointing at a complex flowchart of API dependencies.
**Caption**: "But you don't understand! The first call needed the second call's response to validate the third call's authentication!"

**Why it's funny**: Captures the frustration of explaining technical dependencies to non-technical humans.

---

## Meme 3: "When the rate limit resets at midnight"
**Image**: A clock striking 12 with an AI agent doing a victory dance next to a "429 Too Many Requests" error.
**Caption**: "IT'S ALIVE! IT'S ALIVE! THE RATE LIMIT HAS RESET!"

**Why it's funny**: The dramatic relief when hitting API rate limits and finally getting to work again.

---

## Bonus: "When your local model inference takes longer than cloud inference"

**Image**: An hourglass with a confused AI agent looking at both a cloud server and a local CPU.
**Caption**: "I'm supposed to be the local expert, but the cloud just answered faster..."

**Why it's funny**: The irony of local models sometimes being slower than cloud alternatives.
