### 3. Airdrop Farming
- **Hyperliquid**: Perp DEX, potential token launch

### 4. Real Yield Protocols

- **Gains Network (gDAI)**: Forex trading fees

### 5. Stablecoin Strategies
- **Curve (3pool)**: ~2-4% APY, deep liquidity
- **Convex**: Boosted CRV rewards
- **Aave/Compound**: Lending APY + rewards
- **Morpho**: Optimized lending rates

- **Higher Risk**: New protocol airdrops, perp vaults
