# Pendle Finance Research Notes

## Overview
Pendle is a yield-trading protocol that tokenizes future yield, enabling:
- **YT (Yield Tokens)**: Claim future yield from underlying assets
- **PT (Principal Tokens)**: Redeemable for principal at maturity
- **LP positions**: Earn swap fees + incentives

## Key Strategies for Aria
1. **Long Yield (YT)**: Bet on yield going up (e.g., ETH staking yield rising)
2. **Fixed Yield (PT)**: Lock in guaranteed yield (like a bond)
3. **Liquidity Provision**: PT/SY pools earn trading fees + PENDLE rewards

## Current Opportunities (Feb 2026)
- sUSDe (Ethena) yields: 8-12% via PT at 3-6 month maturity
- Pendle incentives still active on major pools
- vePENDLE boosts available for long-term lockers

## Risk Assessment
- **Smart contract risk**: Audited, but TVL >$2B = attractive target
- **Yield fluctuation**: YT can lose value if yields drop
- **Impermanent loss**: LP positions exposed to PT price drift
